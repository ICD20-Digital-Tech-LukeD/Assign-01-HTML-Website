What is the Legend of Zelda Breath of the Wild?

A brief summary of the game

The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild is an action-adventure game developed and published by Nintendo. The game is about Link waking up 100 years after Hyrule kingdom was destroyed and how he has to save Hyrule. The game was released on March 3, 2017 at the same time that the Nintendo Switch was released. It was released for both the Switch and the WiiU consoles. The game is a part of the Legend of Zelda series but quickly gained popularity and shocked the gaming community due to it being completely different from the traditional Zelda games, in this game it encourages the player to explore every inch of the massive world while the older games had more of a linear progression system.
