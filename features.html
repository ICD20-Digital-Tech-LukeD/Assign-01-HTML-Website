Features

"Breath of the Wild's quality stems from its several new features that set it apart from other Zelda and other action/adventure games. The game's massive open-world environment stands out, offering players freedom to travel and interact with the vast kingdom of Hyrule. Paired with an advanced physics engine, players can interact with the environment in various ways, from climbing steep cliffs to solving puzzles using unique methods. The introduction of different survival mechanics, such as cooking and weather effects, adds lots of depth to gameplay, requiring players to strategize and adapt to their surroundings. Combining this with the game's immersive storytelling, memorable characters, and breathtaking visuals contribute to Breath of the Wild's status as a masterpiece in the world of video games."
