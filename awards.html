The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild Awards

"Breath of the Wild was an enormously popular game that took home numerous awards for its unique design. It received Best Action/Adventure Game and Best Game Design as well as awards from major events like The Game Awards and the Golden Joystick Awards. People loved it because it did things that the Zelda series has never seen before. You could explore a vast open world with an advanced physics engine. Players could do whatever they wanted, which was new and exciting. Both critics and players gave it top ratings because it looked amazing, felt real, and was super fun to play. "
